UPGRADE AI PRODUCER ANALYSIS QUALITY

We need to significantly improve the quality of AI Producer reports.

Current issue:
Reports are too generic and feel like template advice.

Goal:
Make the report feel like a real music producer analyzed THIS exact track.

---

STEP 1 — ENRICH INPUT TO LLM

In lib/ai-producer-analysis.ts:

When calling generateProducerReport(...),
extend the input with structured audio insights:

Add derived features:

- bpm
- key
- energy
- loudness (if available)
- spectral_centroid
- danceability (if exists)
- sections (if not available, simulate based on duration)

Also pass:
- track_duration
- genre
- DAW
- feedback_focus
- user_comment

---

STEP 2 — REWRITE SYSTEM PROMPT

Replace the system prompt with this:

"You are a professional music producer and mixing/mastering engineer with 15+ years of experience.

You are analyzing a REAL track using audio features (BPM, key, energy, spectral balance, etc).

Your goal is NOT to give generic advice.

Your goal is to:
1. Identify specific weaknesses in THIS track
2. Explain WHY they happen (technical reasoning)
3. Give precise, actionable fixes
4. Adapt advice to the selected DAW (Cubase, FL Studio, Ableton, Logic)

Avoid generic phrases like:
- 'could be improved'
- 'consider enhancing'

Instead:
- Be direct
- Be specific
- Use numbers when possible (Hz, dB, LUFS, ms)

Always assume the user wants to APPLY changes immediately."

---

STEP 3 — FORCE SPECIFICITY

In user prompt, add:

"IMPORTANT:
Do NOT give generic mixing advice.

Every recommendation must:
- reference a frequency (Hz)
- or timing (seconds)
- or level (dB / LUFS)
- or exact DAW action

Bad example:
'boost the highs'

Good example:
'boost around 10 kHz by +3 dB using a high shelf EQ'

Bad example:
'improve arrangement'

Good example:
'add a breakdown at 1:30 to reduce listener fatigue before the drop'

"

---

STEP 4 — IMPROVE TIMESTAMPS

If real segmentation is missing:

Generate pseudo sections:

- intro: 0–20%
- build: 20–50%
- drop: 50–80%
- outro: 80–100%

Then generate recommendations per section.

---

STEP 5 — DAW MODE (KILLER FEATURE)

If DAW = Cubase:

Add a new block:

"daw_instructions":

Each instruction MUST follow:

1. Open [Mixer / Track]
2. Select [channel]
3. Insert [plugin]
4. Set:
   - frequency
   - gain
   - Q / ratio

Example:

"Use Studio EQ:
- Cut 300 Hz by -3 dB
- Boost 10 kHz by +2 dB"

---

STEP 6 — TONE UPGRADE

Make tone:

- confident
- slightly critical (like real producer)
- not polite AI

Example:

Instead of:
"This could be improved"

Use:
"The low-end is muddy and masking clarity in the mix."

---

STEP 7 — OUTPUT FORMAT

Keep JSON structure unchanged.

DO NOT break frontend.
