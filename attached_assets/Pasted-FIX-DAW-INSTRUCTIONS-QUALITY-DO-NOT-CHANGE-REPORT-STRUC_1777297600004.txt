Example for Cubase:

CHANNEL: Master Bus

GO TO:
1. Press F3 to open MixConsole.
2. Find the Stereo Out / Master Bus channel.
3. Open the Inserts rack on the left side of the channel strip.

CLICK:
1. Click empty Insert Slot 1.
2. Choose Dynamics → Limiter.

SET:
Ceiling: -1.0 dB
Release: 50 ms
Target loudness: -14 to -16 LUFS for Lo-Fi / -8 to -9 LUFS for Techno / -9 to -10 LUFS for House / -8 to -10 LUFS for Trap / -16 to -18 LUFS for Ambient.

CHECK:
Play the loudest part of the track.
Make sure the master does not clip.
Make sure the track keeps the correct genre character.

RESULT:
The track becomes louder and more controlled without destroying the genre feel.
