Time: 00:00–full track
Category: Mix

Problem:
Kick around 60 Hz and bass around 100 Hz overlap, creating low-end mud.

Why it matters:
The drop loses punch because the kick transient cannot cut through the bass energy.

DAW steps:
1. Open Mixer (F3)
2. Select Bass channel
3. Insert Cubase Frequency EQ
4. Enable high-pass filter at 80 Hz
5. Add sidechain compressor triggered by Kick

Settings:
- HPF: 80 Hz
- Sidechain gain reduction: -4 dB
- Attack: 10 ms
- Release: 120 ms

Expected result:
Cleaner low-end, stronger kick punch, more club-ready drop.
